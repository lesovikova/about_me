A humble hunter

Hey, traveller! You are welcome to sit at my campfire with me. My name is Goringa, I'm a hunter of great skill and renown from the land of Shire which is in Middle-Earth. It's a dark time for all the lands now, for the war with great evil has started and all the free peoples has come together to battle it. I'm a fast traveller and volunteered to get help from other lands and so I'm here.

The land of the Shire has always been a peacefull one and I never expected to leave it. But one evening as I was walking with my friends, we were kidnapped by black riders, I understand now that they mistook us for the hobbits that were travelling with one by the name of Baggins. We were brought to the place near Bree town and probably would have been torchured horribly, but I man called Strider came to our help. We fought side by side to get out of that place and he offered me to enter into a training with some of his brethren. That's how I learned to be a hunter, it was an arduous training, a lot of skills I have aquired only much later. I'm skilled in tracking men, beasts and even evil spirits, can travel great distances extremely fast and of course, skilled with the bow and arrow.

My travels and help with the great battle has taken me to many places, there isn't a land in Eriador where I haven't stepped foot. I'm been to fair Rivendell, have seen the great lake of Evendim, battled foul creatures in Anmar and even went through Moria where it's so easy to get lost. I've become kindred to many peoples and helped them with my skill or just with a good word. My main camp now is in Dol Amroth in Gondor and soon I'll be in a hurry to return thither.
